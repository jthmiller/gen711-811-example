### Project descriptions


## Cyanobacteria
Lake Tahoe Aerosol Project: Pico cyanobacteria that produce toxins may be so small that they are found in water vapor. This might contribute to chronic exposure to biotoxins, and result in pathologies. 

References: doi: https://doi.org/10.3390/ijms22168726, https://doi.org/10.1212/WNL.88.16_supplement.P5.086
AIR-EW, AIR-TK, AIR-VP are aerosolized water samples from each lake (bacteria small enough to aerosolize)
WLW-EW, WLW-TK, WLW-VP are filtered water samples (contains bacteria that would become aerosolized and those that wont)
NTC - No template control
PAC - Blank
